How it Works?

1. Apply for admission at Chui Academy

We know you are excited about the money part, but we must make sure you know what you are doing first.

Before you meet our investment team, you are required to apply and get accepted for admission at Chui Academy, where you will learn best practices from former students who started businesses, raised hundreds of thousands of dollars, and created employment opportunities for others.

  • No boring lectures, but pure storytelling from those who have done it successfully.
  • Gain all the knowledge you need to launch and grow your hustle ahead of graduation day
2. Secure your enrollment ticket

An important quality of any entrepreneur is the ability and willingness to invest in their learning. We ask studentpreneurs to demonstrate this quality by sourcing the admission fee to secure entry into our programs.

  • Enrollment tickets are only available electronically.
  • Every ticket grants you all access at Chui Forums, our online training platform.
3. Pitch To our Campus VCs.

This is our most awaited moment. When you graduate from the academy, you will be given an opportunity to present your idea to a panel of judges composed of investors, students and independent consultants.

  • Every pitch runs for 5 minutes
  • Every pitch is followed by a Q&A session of 5 minutes.
  • We select the top 3 studentpreneurs for funding

And Finally...

4. GET FUNDED.

If you make it this far, congratulations. You will supported by our advisory team to launch and grow your business.

  • Our support is lifelong.
  • We'll help you get featured in mainstream media for heightened exposure
  • We'll facilitate warm introductions to partner investors.
